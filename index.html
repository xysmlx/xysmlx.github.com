Hi, I’m Lingxiao

Researcher @ Computer Systems

I am Lingxiao Ma (马凌霄), a Researcher in Systems and Networking Research Group of Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA). I obtained Ph.D. in Computer Architecture from Peking University in 2020, under supervision of Prof. Yafei Dai. Before that, I obtained B.Sc in Computer Science from Beijing Normal University in 2015. My research works are focused on building efficient parallel systems for large-scale data analytics scenarios, e.g., deep learning, machine learning, graph processing, through leveraging modern hardware like GPU.
